Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Print Something Out?

There are three ways to print recipes on SimplyRecipes.com: 1. by clicking
on the printer icon (grouped with the Facebook, Pinterest, and email icons),
2. by clicking on the “PRINT” button, or 3. by using browser printing.

We recommend options 1 and 2, as both are printer-friendly and usually ranging
between 1 and 3 pages, depending on the length of the recipe. Option 3 still prints
the recipe without photos and ads, however, it tends to use more paper.

Option 1

Computer: At the top of the page on the left of the recipe title and author name.
Click the printer icon (the last icon) to print.

Phone: Scroll down to the middle of the page at the end of the introductory copy.
All the icons are located above the recipe box. Click the printer icon on the far right
to print.

Tablet: All the icons are located at the top of the screen, right above the recipe name.
Click the printer icon on the far right to print.

Where Are the Photos in My Print-Out?

To keep our onsite options printer-friendly, no photos are printed. If you love perusing
through our photos as much as we do or require visual aids, we highly recommend
accessing the recipe online.

Why Has My Favorite Recipe Changed?

We are consistently updating our recipes for an improved reader and home cook experience.
You may notice changes in the form of copy edits, recipe edits, photo edits, or we may even
redirect to a new and improved recipe altogether.
